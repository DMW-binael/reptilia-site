REPTILIANS: BUGS OR ADMINS?

What if reptilians were not extraterrestrials? What if they were the administrators of the simulation?

Every simulation has rules. Laws of physics. Constants. But there are also those who code these rules. Those who can modify them. Those who see the source code.

Ancient legends speak of beings who do not age, who change form, who know the future. Not gods. Developers.

The pyramids? System access points. Reptilian eyes? Glitches visible when the 3D rendering bugs out. Unexplained disappearances? Entities deleted from the database.

If you want to hide a truth, make it a "conspiracy theory". Nobody will take it seriously.

IT IS ONLY A DREAM

Have you ever had a lucid dream? That moment when you realise you are asleep, but the dream continues. That is exactly what someone feels when they awaken inside a simulation.

The difference between a dream and reality? Coherence. But if the simulation is perfectly coherent, how would you know the difference?

Perhaps every night, when you sleep, you wake up in your real life. And what you call "waking up" is actually the moment you reconnect to the simulation.

The t-shirt you are wearing does not exist. These words you are reading do not exist. You do not exist. Not in the way you believe.

You are an idea. A thought. A pattern of information inside a quantum computer from a dimension your simulated brain cannot even begin to conceive.
